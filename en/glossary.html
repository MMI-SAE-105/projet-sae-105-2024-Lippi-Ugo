Glossary

Here you will find a glossary of the complicated terms used in the site's articles.

Insignificant: Without much impact, insignificant.

Avant-garde: Ahead of its time.

Blockbusters: A high-budget film production aimed at producing record profits.

Contextual: Related to the context.

Growing: Growing.

Dilemma: A situation where one must choose between two equally unsatisfactory solutions.

Burdensome: Something heavy to bear, something burdensome.

Proactive: Describes a psychological process that operates from upstream to downstream over time.

Sensory: Related to the senses.

Sophistication: To make more complex, more refined.

Symbiosis: A relationship marked by a very close and harmonious union.
